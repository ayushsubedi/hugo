August-September 2019 Units

Introduction to Probability and Inferential Statistics

This unit covers topics on probability theory, theoritical probability and experimental probability, compound probabilities, conditional probabilities, inferential statistics and correlation and regression.

Introduction to Python and Machine Learning

This unit covers topics on Google Colab for data analysis and machine learning, introduction to Pandas and Seaborn, introduction to machine learning and creating simple machine learning models for regression and classification.

Basic Dataviz with Tableau

This unit covers topics on basic data visualization with Tableau including understanding data, organizing it, and presenting it in formats and visualizations that makes sense to people who use it to make a real impact with data.

Leading a Data Driven Project

This unit covers topics on data collection, planning data driven projects and setting up the team, survey methodologies and collecting data by leveraging a mobile device.

Leadership and Design

This unit covers topics on design thinking and it's application, use of big data in design thinking, servant leadership, presentation skills and the characteristics of a good presentation.
